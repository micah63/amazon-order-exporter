# Privacy Policy — Amazon Order Exporter

**Last updated:** March 26, 2026
**Published by:** Micah Peterson

## What this extension does

Amazon Order Exporter reads your Amazon order history from pages you visit on Amazon and lets you export that data as a CSV file. It also visits Amazon product pages (on the same amazon.com domain you are already browsing) to look up product category information.

## Data collection

This extension does **not** collect any personal data. The developer has no access to your Amazon order information or any other data.

- No data is sent to any external server.
- No analytics or tracking of any kind.
- No cookies are set by the extension.
- No account creation is required.

## Data storage

All order data is stored **locally in your browser** using Chrome's built-in storage (`chrome.storage.local`). The data never leaves your device.

## Network requests

The only network requests the extension makes are to Amazon product pages — the same domain you are already on — to retrieve product category information. No data is sent to any other server or third party.

## Deleting your data

You can delete all stored data at any time by clicking the "Clear All" button in the extension. You can also remove the extension entirely, which will delete all associated data from your browser.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date.
