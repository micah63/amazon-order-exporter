Description

Ever wish you could just get your Amazon purchases into a spreadsheet? Now you can. Amazon Order Exporter grabs your order history - item names, prices, dates, and categories - and turns it into a clean spreadsheet file you can open in Excel, Google Sheets, or any budgeting app. HOW IT WORKS: 1. Go to your Amazon order history 2. Click "Collect Orders" on each page 3. Hit "Download CSV" and you're done That's it. No setup, no account, no learning curve. WHAT YOU GET: • Item names and prices • Order dates and totals • Product categories (like "Books", "Electronics", "Grocery") • Everything organized and ready to import YOUR DATA IS SAFE: • Nothing ever leaves your browser - period • No servers, no sign-ups, no tracking • We don't see your data. Nobody does. It stays on your computer. • The code is 100% open source - anyone can read it at github.com/micah63/amazon-order-exporter BUILT FOR BUDGETING: Works great with icanbudget and any app or spreadsheet that opens CSV files. Finally see where your Amazon money actually goes. Works with Amazon US and Canada.
